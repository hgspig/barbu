Our mission
Our mission is to help students keep track of what they need to do each day. Our design is simple in order to keep distractions low and productivity high. Notifications to and from peers when tasks are created or completed increase accountability and motivation. Ultimately we are providing a way for students to stay on task, become more productive and get more done in less time.
